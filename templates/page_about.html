About

{ Brief History }

I am a mobile and web application developer, and a lifelong fan of "pen and paper" RPGs. After noticing how much time I was "playing" Facebook and Foursquare on my mobile phone throughout the day; it occurred to me that I'd rather use all that time and all those friends to role play. So I began developing a web service that would power d20 game mechanics and Open Game Content for mobile devices.

My goal was to leverage geo-location and connected mobile devices to create a parallel reality where players could battle monsters, navigate puzzles and traps, meet other player characters, find treasure and build communities/factions. All game events would be logged and used to create a fantasy map overlay on the real world Google map.

The result? Your favorite coffee shop also becomes the place where you and a handful of other heroes battled the undead (and won). Your favorite park down the street is full of Orcs and as a consequence has become a magnet for adventurers seeking glory (and many only finding their end).

Life is short, so why not fill it with as much adventure as you can?

{ A Kickstarter Project }

I began developing d20 Morningstar in March 2011. As of June 2011, a stable version of the game server is complete and is being tested with a prototype mobile client built in HTML5. The next step is to build a native Android app, which will be the first released version of the game, and available for free.

Morningstar development is self-funded: I have been working full time on the project, spending my savings and using credit cards to pay bills. I need help from the community to keep me working on this project and see it through to completion.

The Kickstarter model is ideal for this project:

  • Those interested in the game can support the project for as little as $5.
  • Backers of the project will receive cool rewards.
  • The funding is all or nothing -- if the goal isn't met, then no one loses any money.
  • The API will be Open Source, allowing other developers to build games and apps.

Please check out the rewards offered on d20 Morningstar's Kickstarter project page. Support the project if you can, and share with friends.

I hope to see you in game!

-- Will Merydith
